Series Description

Dark-Hunter Series

An urban paranormal fantasy romance series about a cadre of immortal warriors who protect mankind from evil, Sherrilyn Kenyon¿s Dark-Hunter novels are sexy and fast-paced with multi-layered plots, engaging characters, and thrilling suspense. Night Pleasures is the first Dark-Hunter book in this ongoing series. Kenyon is also the author of the paranormal romance series the League, and the young-adult series, Chronicles of Nick.

Meet the Author

Sherrilyn Kenyon
Sherrilyn Kenyon has created her own universe, populated by all manner of preternatural beings. After selling her first book Born of Night in 1995, her catalog has since expanded to over a dozen internationally bestselling series. Her image-driven writing gives each of her paranormal romances a cinematic quality that betrays her interest in comics.
