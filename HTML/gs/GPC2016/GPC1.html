Join us in Stores July 6th – August 7th

It’s time for Get Pop-Cultured, Barnes & Noble’s annual celebration of fanboys, fangirls, and pop-culture. From Finding Dory to Star Wars™, DC™ to Marvel®, Pokémon™ to Comic Convention Collectibles, and Manga to the return of Harry Potter, your local B&N store has you covered. Come in to experience an entire month of family-friendly activities, games, giveaways (while supplies last), trivia, special promotions, cosplay, and more! Check with your local store for details.

Discover events near you

Disney/Pixar Finding Dory

Friday July 8th, 7PM Come dive into the fun as we celebrate all things Dory, the world’s most loveable, forgetful fish. Experience the magic of the new Finding Dory movie with a scavenger hunt, crafts, and coloring activities to entertain the entire family, plus free giveaways (while supplies last), including posters, stickers, and bookmarks.

Star Wars™

Friday July 15th, 7PM You won't need to journey to a galaxy far, far away for an evening of Star Wars-themed fun, including a trivia contest, Star Wars bingo, coloring activities, comic and poster giveaways (while supplies last), a learn-to-play Star Wars X-Wing™ Game event and the best cosplay this side of Jakku.

Pokémon™

Saturday July 16th, 4PM Celebrate 20 years of Pokémon! Snap a photo with a Pikachu and Charizard standee and learn trading card game strategies to aid you on your quest to become a Pokémaster.
Enjoy a special offer, in stores only.

Manga

Saturday July 16th, 7PM Celebrate the world of Japanese comics with draw and color manga activities from renowned artist Camilla d’Errico. Enjoy cosplay and exciting giveaways (while supplies last) including manga samplers. Plus, celebrate the launch of exclusive Tokidoki products with free buttons and trading cards. Build out your manga library with a special offer from July 6-19.

Comic Convention Collectibles

Thursday July 21st - Sunday July 24th Don’t miss your chance to pick up limited edition, in-demand collectibles from your favorite pop-culture brands, including Diamond™ Collectibles and the addictive Pop! figures from Funko. These collectibles will be available starting July 21st, while supplies last. Limit 2 per customer. Warning: These will sell out—fast.

DC™

Saturday July 23rd, 7PM Explore the DC universe! Be first in line for a free Harley Quinn and the Suicide Squad #1 Special Edition Comic giveaway (while supplies last), show off your superhero skills with cosplay and coloring activities, and enjoy a special offer on DC Graphic Novels from July 20-24.

Countdown to Midnight Magic

Saturday July 30th - Sunday July 31st It’s a magical time to love Harry Potter! Share your Harry Potter memories on our Muggle Wall and stay for a night of activities, cosplay, trivia, and giveaways (while supplies last) leading up to the midnight release of Harry Potter and the Cursed Child parts I & II. Learn more about upcoming Harry Potter events at B&N.

Marvel®

Saturday August 6th, 7PM Suit up and explore the world of Marvel comics! Cosplay as your favorite hero, pick up exclusive comic samplers, including the first storyline for Mosaic (while supplies last), prove you're a trivia superhero, and enjoy coloring activities. Receive a special offer on Marvel graphic novels from July 25 - August 7.
