Join us in Stores July 6th – August 7th

It’s time for Get Pop-Cultured, Barnes & Noble’s annual celebration of fanboys, fangirls, and pop-culture. From Finding Dory to Star Wars™, DC™ to Marvel®, Pokémon™ to Comic Convention Collectibles, and Manga to the return of Harry Potter, your local B&N store has you covered. Come in to experience an entire month of family-friendly activities, games, giveaways (while supplies last), trivia, special promotions, cosplay, and more! Check with your local store for details.

Calendar of Events

Friday July 8th, 7PM Extend the magic of the new Finding Dory movie with a scavenger hunt, crafts, and coloring activities, plus free giveaways (while supplies last) including posters, stickers, and bookmarks.

Star Wars™

Friday July 15th, 7PM You won’t need to journey to a galaxy far, far away for an evening of Star Wars-themed fun, including cosplay, trivia, Star Wars bingo, coloring activities, and giveaways (while supplies last). 

Pokémon™

Saturday July 16th, 4PM Celebrate 20 years of Pokémon! Snap a photo with a Pikachu and Charizard standee and learn trading card game strategies to aid you on your quest to become a Pokémaster. Enjoy a special offer, in stores only.

Manga

Comic Convention Collectibles

DC™

Saturday July 23rd, 7PM Be first in line for a free Harley Quinn and the Suicide Squad #1 Special Edition giveaway (while supplies last), show off your superhero skills with cosplay and coloring activities, and enjoy a special offer on DC graphic novels.

Countdown to Midnight Magic

Saturday July 30th - Sunday July 31st Share your memories on our Muggle Wall and enjoy a night of activities and giveaways (while supplies last) leading up to the midnight release of the new book. Learn more about upcoming Harry Potter events at B&N.

Marvel®

Saturday August 6th, 7PM Soar into the Marvel comics universe! Cosplay as your favorite hero, pick up exclusive comic samplers (while supplies last), and enjoy trivia, coloring activities, and a special offer on Marvel graphic novels.
